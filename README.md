<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

This project is an end-to-end Data Analytics portfolio project that demonstrates how raw organizational data can be transformed into meaningful business insights using Python, SQL, Power BI, and structured reporting.

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

Effective agreement and billing management are critical for maintaining financial health and ensuring timely revenue realization. This project aims to address the following business challenges:- Identify underperforming brands needing pricing or promotional adjustments
- Identify agreements and issuers with high outstanding billing balances that may impact cash flow
- Determine top revenue-contributing issuers and agreements
- Statistically validate differences in vendor profitability
- Monitor AMC, Security, and EMD performance to ensure accurate financial tracking
- Highlight agreements with inefficient billing cycles or low realization rates

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="dataset"></a>Dataset</h2>

The dataset includes information related to:

- Tender details (issuer, quantity, rate, total amount)
- Agreement lifecycle (start date, completion date, remarks)
- Billing performance (gross amount, tax, deductions, net amount, received & balance)
- AMC, Security, and EMD financial tracking
